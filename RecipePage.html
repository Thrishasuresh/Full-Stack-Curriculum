Delicious Recipe

This is a simple and delicious recipe that you can easily make at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the flour, eggs, milk, and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Pour the mixture into a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
